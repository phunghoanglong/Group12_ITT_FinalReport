A. Aim

The aim of our project is to make it simple for our customers to find and buy the gym supplement, equipment, and clothes that best meet their requirements. Most people have a very hard time finding the things that support their fitness objectives. Our website provides a user-friendly platform, packed with pictures and smooth animation that will make it very easy for customers to browse, read reviews and get to choose the product that is best for them.

Our goal is to create a website that is easy to navigate,with detailed product descriptions, high-quality images, and a safe online payment system.Our website is divided into several parts, ranging from supplements to equipment to apparel and PT packs. They are carefully isolated and made as accessible as feasible. Each product will have a separate page with extensive information and pricing to let customers pick what they want.

Making our website as sleek as we can will attract visitors to stay on it longer. Our website is designed in dark mode to make it easier to view for extended periods of time. Each feature has been carefully designed to avoid conflict with others. Customers can find every service they require under one roof. This is highly important because a decent website will encourage users to stay on it longer, increasing the likelihood that they will purchase our product.

As a group of four members with no prior experience in Information Technology, we started planning to build a website to improve the personal experience. However, we are uncertain about the medical knowledge and Khai said that fitness has become more popular so we decided to make a commercial website for the final idea with a personalizing feature based on the unique physical, favorite and financial needs of each individual user. Although fitness websites out there with high reputations are our challenges, noticing that they do not highly optimize the customer experience, we believed that our website would be valuable to the fitness community, as it would cater to the specific needs and goals of each user.

In order to begin the development of our website, we have planned and set some objectives for ourselves. First and foremost, our top priority is to focus on the core functionalities such as the login feature, which is crucial in optimizing user experience. We understand that this feature is the backbone of the website and it needs to be seamless and easy to use. Second, we aim to create visually appealing home pages, search bars, and tables of contents that will make navigation more intuitive for users. These pages will be designed to be user-friendly and easy to navigate so that users can easily find what they're looking for. By focusing on these objectives, we hope to make sure that our website will be an enjoyable and seamless experience for all users.

After discussing, we immediately seek ways to program the website along with database processing.
